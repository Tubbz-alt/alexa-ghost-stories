How To Use

There are many ways to invoke the Ghost Stories skill from your Alexa-enabled device.

"Alexa, open Ghost Stories."

Activating the skill will give you any updates recently released.

"I need help."

Invoking the help function in Ghost Stories, will provide you with some commands to get you started.

"Alexa, tell me a Ghost Stories, I want to be frightened."

Let Alexa randomly select which story to tell.

"Alexa, tell me a Ghost Stories to tell me a story."

Alexa will find a scary story from list of contributed tales.
